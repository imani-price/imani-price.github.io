༘ More♡

A large majority of the healthcare in Africa is focused on curing disease. This is because due to the poverty and unclean living conditions of people, it is easy to catch and send diseases to different people.

The diseases that mostly affect Africa are malaria and HIV, however diseases like cancer, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease are quickly catching up.

A few ways to help address these problems according to taeudu.org are: Incorporating Technology into Healthcare, Improving Doctor Patient Ratio, Giving Public Awareness
