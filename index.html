Healthcare is a problem faced widely around the world. Either it is too expensive for some to afford, or the quality is poor. Every single person desrves to recieve the best healthcare no matter their status, or location of living.

༘Problem♡

A good example of this is Africa. In Africa, healthcare isn't as best as it could be. Which is why many individuals suffer and pass away due to diseases and viruses. In most countries healthcare is really poor, which is why people would rather go to witch doctors and find other solutions of their own. Not only this but due to the immense amount of poverty faced in Africa, not many can afford the large bills the hosptals and doctors dish out. All of this connects to form a cycle. A sick person goes to a doctor, the doctor isn't good or they can't afford the doctor, they go to elsewhere to find solutions, but those solutions are never the answer.

༘Solutions♡

To help with problem, systems need to be put in place to make sure every person can access the meedical care they need and desrve. Instead of stealing money, goverments should put it towards building hospitals and advancing the tehnology and medicines needed in them. Mobile healthcare trucks are much less expensive and healthcare, and can be accessed by people in rural areas who don't live near hospitals and clinics.
